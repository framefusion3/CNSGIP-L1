Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, rich, and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  3. Cook pancetta or bacon until crispy, then add cooked spaghetti to the pan.
  4. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture until creamy.
  5. Serve hot with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper.

Chicken Tikka Masala

This popular Indian dish features tender chicken in a creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Cream

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or bake chicken until cooked through.
  3. In a separate pan, simmer tomato sauce with cream and additional spices.
  4. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer until heated through.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
